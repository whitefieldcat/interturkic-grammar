\chapter{Latin}

The Interturkic Latin alphabet consists of 34 letters, 33 of which have both
upper- and lowercase forms.
Each letter is listed in table~\ref{tbl:latin-alphabet}, including their names,
phonemic values, and forms.
There is an exact one\-/to\-/one correspondence between underlying phonemes and
written letters.
